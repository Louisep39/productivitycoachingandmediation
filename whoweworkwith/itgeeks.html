CIOs & VPs IT Geeks

IT Geeks

Use short-term consulting and long-term coaching to help you:
  • Get clients to understand you, and even admire you.
  • Take the stress and dread out of making presentations and talking directly to clients.
  • Get management to respect your ideas and expertise, and to act on your recommendations.
  • Be able to do good work on problems that fascinate you.
  • Become "outward facing" and "customer focused" and (gasp) even enjoy it.

Work with a consultant and coach who speaks precisely and thinks logically, and doesn't use fluffy jargon or "woo woo" terms.

Offerings

Advance your career with coaching to help you let your good ideas be heard; and to confidently make the transition to feel comfortable talking to clients, and proposing products and services that benefit them.

Short-Term Consultation
Individual coaching for a particular pitch or situation. Three sessions per month, 45 minutes each, by phone, Skype, or messaging. E-mail consultation in between sessions. One to three months, $400 per month.

On-Going Coaching
Individual ongoing coaching. Three sessions per month, 45 minutes each, by phone, Skype, or messaging. E-mail consultation in between sessions. Minimum of four months. $1,200 for the first four months, then $375 per month.
