Reengage key talent by enhancing important work relationships.

Case Study: The Difficult Employee

An IT manager is dealing with a formerly excellent, productive team member whose engagement is dwindling. Their client is angry and team morale is dropping, but letting her go would incur the expense of hiring and on-boarding. With coaching through a mediation session, the manager and team member come up with concrete strategies to re-engage her in the company.

Case Study: The Difficult Employee

If you have important personnel who are not working effectively together, save yourself the time and expense of finding and on-boarding new talent by restoring your talent's ability to work effectively together.

In mediation, the individuals involved have a private problem solving session with a trained mediator to coach them through a process of mending their work relationship, and setting a positive and productive roadmap to the future.
