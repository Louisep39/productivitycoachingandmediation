Why Work With Us

Unlike other trainers, mediators, and coaches, I communicate clearly, concretely, and systematically. Whether I'm leading a training, mediating a workplace dispute, or helping IT people prosper in the changing world of IT, I organize the information and structure the work logically.

If you're an IT person, you can relax and be comfortable — and even enthusiastic (really!) — knowing that I appreciate your specificity and logical way of thinking. Unlike others using "soft skills" to help people in IT, I get you because I'm like you.

If you're a CIO or VP, or in HR, your IT talent will get more out of working with me than with trainers, mediators, and coaches who don't understand them. Your talent will report greater satisfaction and achieve better outcomes.
