The Difficult Employee

An IT manager is dealing with a formerly excellent, productive team member whose engagement is dwindling. Their client is angry and team morale is dropping, but letting her go would incur the expense of hiring and on-boarding. With coaching through a mediation session, the manager and team member come up with concrete strategies to re-engage her in the company.
