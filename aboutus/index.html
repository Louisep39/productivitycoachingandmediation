About Us

Who I am

I'm Louise Penberthy, coach, trainer, and mediator.


Who I work with

I work with IT departments who are transitioning to become
  • customer-facing
  • creators of products and services
  • contributors to the company's ROI

Unlike other coaches, trainers, and mediators, I

  • understand IT people, because I am one
  • have transitioned my own mind-set the way your talent needs to
  • communicate in a way that won't leave your talent complaining about "fluffy" jargon or "woo-woo" concepts
  • help you resolve the conflicts that the changing alignment of IT will bring
  • use my theater background to help your talent speak easefully, persuasively, and fearlessly

My Background

In my 11 years as a mediator, I have coached hundreds of people of different backgrounds to communicate effectively and resolve conflicts. I have 17 years of experience in the IT and web industries as a project manager, programmer, interface designer, client, and consultant. In those 17 years, I worked in many situations: as an employee and contractor, and for companies and non-profits of all kinds: big, small, successful, and crash-and-burn.
